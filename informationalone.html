How terms are written and what they mean

While encountering algebra there are special notations that aren't normal to standard mathematics, when an expression is wrote as "9x" this means in standard mathematics 9 times x, which may be confusing at first but it will stick after a few attempts.

Also be careful with your BIDMAS and BODMAS, equations can be simplified down when using algebra so using the correct order of elimination and combination is important, for example with the equation x + x + (x - 3) + (x - 3) this would simpify down to 4x - 6, this is because the calculations inside the brackets are done first, because you can't alter a variable they stay more or less the same and when the -3 + -3 is calculated in basic mathematical rules when a + % - collide they minus from each other creating the -6 then the remainder is the 4x's.
